Must visit places in Almaty

  • Shymbulak Ski Resort

Shymbulak Ski Resort, nestled in the stunning Trans-Ili Alatau mountains, offers world-class skiing and snowboarding with breathtaking alpine views. Perfect for all skill levels, it features modern facilities, cozy lodges, and year-round activities. Visitors can enjoy fresh mountain air, thrilling slopes, and easy access, making it a must-visit destination.

  • Big Almaty Lake

Big Almaty Lake, nestled in the Tian Shan Mountains, is a stunning alpine gem surrounded by snow-capped peaks. Its turquoise waters change hues with the seasons, creating a breathtaking sight. Perfect for nature lovers, the lake offers serene landscapes, fresh mountain air, and nearby hiking trails for an unforgettable adventure.
